ColorBrawl

What is ColorBrawl?

ColorBrawl is a simple PVP-orientated minigame, the goal is to drain the opponent from their health. But unlike conventional PVP games, their health isn't displayed
as hearts, but rather wool blocks which make up their "land". Everytime you get hit some of your blocks will change to white wool, until you've got nothing left.
To win the game you'll need to drain the health of each opponent.

Each time you land a hit on another player, you'll regrain health; Depending on the strength on the attack. The more critical the hit, the more health you'll recieve.
Each player also recieves three golden apples, which when consumed will restore 50 health.
